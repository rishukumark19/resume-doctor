About Me

With over a decade of surgical experience, my professional life is devoted to the art and science of healing through surgery. My foundation in medicine was built at the Johns Hopkins School of Medicine, a journey that has led me to practice in high-volume, academically-oriented medical centers. My focus remains steadfast on delivering meticulous surgical technique while advancing the field through clinical research and mentorship.

My approach to patient care is rooted in a deep-seated belief in a sacred trust. I ensure every patient and their family fully understands the proposed procedure, its risks, and its benefits, fostering a partnership in their journey to recovery. I specialize in minimally invasive laparoscopic and robotic-assisted procedures, which reduce recovery time and improve outcomes for complex abdominal and soft tissue conditions.
